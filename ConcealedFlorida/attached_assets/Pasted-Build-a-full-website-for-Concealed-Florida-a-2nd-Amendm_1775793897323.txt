Build a full website for "Concealed Florida" — a 2nd Amendment education and 
community platform. The site has 3 main pillars with the motto: 
"We Are Ready. We Are Watching. We Are Hiding in Plain Sight."
Mascot: Florida Panther. Dark tactical aesthetic.

--- SITE STRUCTURE ---

NAVBAR: Home | We Are Ready | We Are Watching | We Are Hiding in Plain Sight | Contact

---

PAGE 1: WE ARE READY
Sub-tabs: Fitness | First Aid & Skills | Mental Preparedness

[FITNESS TAB]
- Display fitness standards in a card/table layout by demographic:
  Boys (under 13), Teens (13-17), Young Adults (18-25), Adults (26-39), 40+
- For each group show: push-ups, sit-ups, pull-ups, 2-mile run time
- Source: US Army ACFT standards (public domain), USMC PFT, Navy PRT
- Two tiers: "Civilian Baseline" and "Military Readiness Standard"
- Button: "Advanced Training" → leads to a resources page with embedded 
  YouTube videos (Jeff Nippard, Alan Thrall, etc.) with title + description 
  + benefit + link. No autoplay.

[FIRST AID TAB]
- Cards linking to free resources: Stop the Bleed, FEMA, Red Cross
- Embed or link YouTube: tourniquet application, CPR, wound packing

---

PAGE 2: WE ARE WATCHING
Sub-tabs: Crime & Awareness Map | News & Laws | Hurricane Preparedness

[MAP TAB]
- Embed the S2 Underground CIP map via iframe:
  https://nexus-s2underground.hub.arcgis.com/maps/7f13eda1f301431e98a7ac0393b0e6b0/explore
- Credit S2 Underground with link and description below map
- Add a note: "We do not own this map. Full credit to S2 Underground."
- Optionally layer a Crimemapping.com embed for Florida local crime data
- Add sex offender overlay using free Florida FDLE public data iframe:
  https://offender.fdle.state.fl.us/offender/sops/home.jsf

[NEWS & LAWS TAB]
- RSS feed auto-fetching 2nd Amendment news using RSS2JSON API (free tier)
  Sources: NRA-ILA (nraila.org/feed), Bearing Arms, The Truth About Guns
- Display as cards: headline, source, date, link to original
- Past stories accumulate in a scrollable list (store in localStorage or 
  a free Supabase DB)

[HURRICANE TAB]
- Email signup form → connect to Mailchimp free tier (500 contacts free)
- Show NHC active storm tracker embed: 
  https://www.nhc.noaa.gov
- FEMA preparedness checklist displayed as interactive checklist
- Basic supply list with Amazon affiliate links (optional — free to set up)

---

PAGE 3: WE ARE HIDING IN PLAIN SIGHT
Sub-tabs: Holsters & Carry | EDC Gear | Clothing & Blending In

[HOLSTERS TAB]
Sub-categories: Appendix (AIWB) | OWB | IWB | Body Type Recommendations
- Cards per product: image, name, brand (945 Industries, Arrowhead Tactical, etc.)
  short review, "Best for: [body type]", link to product page
- Embedded YouTube review video per product with description

[EDC GEAR TAB]
- Same card layout: lights, knives, med kits, communication
- Links to reviewed products with editorial note that there is no paid promotion

[CLOTHING TAB]
- Tips on concealment-friendly clothing brands
- Cards with images, descriptions, links
